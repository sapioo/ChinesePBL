The Ancient Four Pillars
四柱命理

The Chinese Zodiac is not merely about years. It is a complex metaphysical system known as the 'Ba Zi', where your birth hour, day, and month contribute to your elemental alignment.

Each sign is also associated with one of the Five Elements: Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water. Together, they create a 60-year cycle of infinite variations.
